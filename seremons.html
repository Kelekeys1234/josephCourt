Joseph's Court Ministers

Pastor Favour Olutayo
Pastor in Charge of Parish/Zone

Favour, is a dynamic and vibrant young man with a golden heart for the saints of God and the masses at large. A nation builder and a strong believer of New Nigeria, he has been Pastoring in The Redeemed Christian Church of God since 2007, a believer in the total gospel of Jesus Christ, (Holiness, Deliverance, Prosperity, Healing and Rapture), these he has preached in many places both in Nigeria and United State of America (USA) and the Lord has shown Himself strong. He is a graduate of Mass Communications from the prestigious University of Nigeria (UNN), with a brilliant background in printing Technology from Yaba College of Technology and later obtained his Master’s in Business Administration (MBA) IN Ajayi Crowther University respectively. He also had several leadership training and conferences both in Nigeria, Europe and America. A graduate of Bible College and School of Discipleship in The Redeemed Christian Church of God. He is currently a Board member of many organizations from Printing Industry, to Finance company and Medical sector to mention a few. He is currently a Pastor in charge of Zone 005 in Youth Province 1 in the mission (RCCG). Favour is happily married to his heartthrob, Olori Shola Olutayo and they are blessed with 3 lovely Prince and Princesses to the glory of God.

Dr. Shola Olutayo
Wife of the Pastor in charge and Zonal Director of women Affairs / Junior church

Dr Olutayo Oritsesholaye Stella an experience Health Administrator with 16 years’ experience in delivery of health care services and preventive health through medical mission within Nigeria and Health care resourcing and supply chain management. Currently she works as a consultant delivering specific primary health programs in communities in Nigeria and has also worked with non-governmental organisations including faith-based organizations. She has been at the fore-front of cancer screening, treatment, and prevention initiatives accross the country since 2009 and with her team has screened more than 20 million women. She holds a Bachelor of Medicine and Surgery degree from the University of Lagos with an elective in Breast Radiology from the Kings College London. Over the years, She has attended a series of Management seminars and training programs geared toward the effective management of personnel and capital resources in the Nigeria, Uk and the United State of America She is currently undergoing a postgraduate course leading to the award of Executive Masters in Public Health of the University of Lagos. Married to her lovely husband and her prayer partner Pastor Favour Olutayo and blessed with Children. Dr Olutayo is a strong believer in the rights of the Nigerian woman to comprehensive health care including the right for a girl to say "No to female circumcision" with attendant genital mutilation which unfortunately is deeply entrenched in the customs and traditions of many parts of Nigeria.
